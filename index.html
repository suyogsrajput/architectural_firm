Services

Architecture


We believe that the best architecture results from a synthesis of all the elements that separate and inform the character of a building: the structure it holds; the services that make it work; its ecology; the quality of natural light; the symbolism of form; the relationship of the building to the skyline or the street scene; the way you move through or around it; and last but not least, his ability to raise the spirits. Through creative collaboration from the start of a project, architects and engineers combine their knowledge to develop integrated, sustainable design solutions.

Structural Engineering


The structural engineering team has experience in tall and complex buildings and special knowledge in non-linear and seismic planning and analysis. The team works together with the teams of architects on projects from the initial concept through to completion on site. They also work with other engineering firms on projects where knowledge of local third-party regulations and procedures can be beneficial.

Sustainability


The Sustainability Group acts as a catalyst for thought leadership in practice and is looking for ways to advance our sustainable design processes. Working directly with the design teams, it provides insights, provides instructions, and measures improvements to develop a sustainable vision for projects that are carried out in practice.
