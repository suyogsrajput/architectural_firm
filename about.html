ABOUT

Developing new projects for a better and sustainable future

S & D Architects' was founded in 2018 by Ar. Shruti Khare and Ar. Dipankar Bagde. Based in Bhopal, we have a global reach and offer solutions to contemporary architectural and urban problems in all regions of the world. Our highly collaborative, research-based design method involves customers, stakeholders and experts from a wide range of fields early on in the creative process. The result is exemplary, outspoken projects that enable our cities and landscapes to move towards a better future.
